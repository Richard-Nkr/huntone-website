Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

HuntOne collects the following information to provide and improve the service:

  • Account information: Email address, username, and authentication provider (Apple, Google, or email).
  • Profile information: Avatar image and display name (optional).
  • Photos: Images you capture or upload within the app to compose your daily grid frames.
  • Published frames: When you choose to publish a frame, it becomes visible to other users in the social feed, along with your username and the city associated with the frame.
  • Location data: We use your approximate location solely to determine your city name for published frames. Precise GPS coordinates are never stored or shared.
  • Social data: Friends list, likes, and comments you make on published frames.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To display your profile (username, avatar) to other users
  • To publish your frames in the social feed when you choose to share them
  • To determine your city name for published frames (location lookup only, no coordinates stored)
  • To facilitate social features: friends, likes, and comments

3. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

HuntOne uses Supabase as its cloud backend provider. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase's privacy practices can be reviewed at supabase.com/privacy.

Photos are stored locally on your device. Published frame images are uploaded to Supabase Storage and made publicly accessible via the feed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your published frames, username, and avatar are visible to other HuntOne users through the social feed. Unpublished photos and grids remain private on your device.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us (see Section 7). Upon account deletion:

  • Your profile, published frames, comments, and likes are removed from our servers
  • Your local data (photos, grids) remains on your device until you delete the app

6. Children's Privacy

HuntOne is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to delete your data, contact us at:

Email: privacy@huntone.online

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
