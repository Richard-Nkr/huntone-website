How it works

1

A Color a Day

Every day, Huntone generates a unique color just for you — from thousands of possibilities. No two days are alike.

2

Hunt & Capture

Take 9 photos featuring that color around you. A door, a flower, a sign — anything. Crop and adjust each tile.

3

Build Your Grid

Your 9 photos become a beautiful 3×3 frame. Add your city and publish.

4

Share & Discover

Share your frame anywhere. Discover other hunters' grids in the feed. Like, comment, make friends.
